FINGO

FINGO is a fun packed online multiplayer game. It is very similar to the commonly played Bingo, with a pinch of mathematics to kindle your brain.

How to play?

  1. Sign up with a fancy name.

  2. Login.

  3. Create a room and share the code with your friends.

  4. FINGO cards have 25 numbers generated randomly.

  5. Each player gets a chance to call out a number(any number). All the players(including the caller) can strike one number on their card,
     if its same as the called number or
     if its a factor of the called number or
     if its a multiple of the called number

  6. Here's where the 'flash' inside you should come in since you'll have only 15 seconds to strike.

  7. If it's your chance to call out a number, u'll have to do it in 10 seconds. Otherwise we'll automaticlly generate a number for u and u'll lose the oppurtunity to create an advantage.

  8. Scoring is based on,
      5 points for striking a number.
      20 points for striking all numbers in a row/column.
      25 points for striking all numbers in a diagonal.
  9. The first person who strikes all the numbers in their card wins.

  10. No more instructions. Its time to rock and roll!!
